Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Keith E. Vaughan, DDS

Dr. Vaughan has been proudly serving York County and the surrounding Yorktown community since 2008. A graduate of Virginia Commonwealth University's School of Dentistry, he brings a philosophy of clear communication, clinical excellence, and genuine concern for every patient's well-being.

Whether you need a routine cleaning or a complete smile transformation, Dr. Vaughan and his experienced team take the time to understand your goals and make every visit comfortable and stress-free.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From preventive care to advanced cosmetic treatments — all under one roof, for patients of every age.

Preventive Care

Regular exams, professional cleanings, digital X-rays, and oral cancer screenings to keep your smile healthy for life.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain veneers, professional teeth whitening, dental bonding, and smile makeovers tailored to your vision.

Restorative Dentistry

Crowns, bridges, dental implants, and tooth-colored fillings to restore full function and a natural appearance.

Invisalign® Clear Aligners

Straighten your smile discreetly with custom clear aligners — no metal wires or brackets required.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements that restore your confidence and chewing ability.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies don't wait for business hours. Call us — we do our best to see urgent cases the same day.
